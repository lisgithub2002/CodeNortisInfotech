  • Python Programming: Proficiency in Python, commonly used for AI and machine learning projects.

  • Data Visualization: Basic skills in data visualization tools for analyzing data and model performance.
  • Statistical Analysis: Understanding of statistical methods for evaluating models and performance.
  • Teamwork & Communication: Ability to collaborate effectively in cross-functional teams and communicate technical concepts.
  • Continuous Learning: Willingness to stay current with advancements in AI and machine learning.
